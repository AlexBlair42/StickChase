Stick Chase

Created by Alex Blair and Andrew McNeill

This game features a stick man named Phillip Stickman who is in dire need to escape the evil Eraser of Death.
It is a platform game where Phillip is being chased by an eraser and must avoid all obstacles in his way.
Will Phillip prevail? Only you, the player can determine that!
